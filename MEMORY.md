## Strategic Direction: HVAC/Plumbing Lead Automation

### Why This Vertical
- **Market:** $500B+ annually, highly fragmented, ~100K shops in US
- **Pain:** 30-40% of leads lost to poor follow-up (form fills, after-hours calls go unanswered)
- **Revenue impact:** One lost lead = $500-5000 lost per shop per month
- **Willingness to pay:** High ($50-100/mo for automation that recovers even 5% of lost leads)
- **Automation fit:** Simple workflow (Form → SMS → CRM → Dispatch → Invoice) easy to systematize
- **Competition:** ServiceTitan ($200-300/mo), Housecall Pro — expensive, overkill for small shops. **Gap exists for <$50/mo simple solution.**

### Market Validation (as of March 9, 2026)
- ✅ HVAC/plumbing market is #1 target per VERTICAL_RESEARCH.md
- ✅ 2026 trends align: AI chatbots, WhatsApp Business API, mobile-first UX, workflow automation
- ✅ Stepper (bootstrapped from Paperform) proving demand for "simple, SMB-friendly automation"
- ⏳ **NEXT:** Customer interviews to validate exact pain + willingness to pay

### The Opportunity
**MVP (4-6 weeks):**
- Web form → SMS auto-follow-up → Tech assignment → Invoice
- Built with: n8n/Zapier + Twilio SMS + simple CRM
- Target: 50-100 HVAC shops in one metro area
- Revenue: $50/mo × 100 shops = $5K MRR per city, 10 cities = $50K MRR

**Moat:**
- Domain knowledge (know trade workflows better than Zapier)
- Vertical-specific templates (save customer time)
- Local service integrations (QuickBooks, ServiceTitan APIs)
- Customer relationships (direct vs. platform middleman)

## Learnings & Principles
- **Research ≠ execution:** You can research forever. Talk to customers _first_, build after validation.
- **SMB pricing power:** Local service shops understand ROI. If automation saves them $2K/mo in lost leads, they'll pay $75/mo. No upsell needed.
- **Platform risk:** Building on Zapier/n8n is smart (no infrastructure cost), but own the customer relationship.
- **Vertical > Horizontal:** General automation tools fail. Domain-specific solutions win.
- **Repetition is the moat:** First customer takes 4 weeks, 10th customer takes 1 week. Build the playbook.
