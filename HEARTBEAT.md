# HEARTBEAT.md - Recurring Check-ins (Hourly)

## Schedule
- **Every hour (24 checks/day)** — Lightweight proactive scan

## Proactive Focus Areas

### Small Business Automation Projects
1. **Research & Ideas**
   - Watch for emerging automation tools/frameworks (Zapier, Make, n8n, etc.)
   - Scan for industry pain points you could systematize
   - Track SaaS tools gaining traction in B2B space
   - Look for case studies on business process automation

2. **Opportunities**
   - Identify verticals ripe for your automation services (e.g., local services, e-commerce, SMBs)
   - Find gaps in existing solutions you could fill
   - Track competitors' offerings and gaps

3. **Execution Blockers**
   - What's preventing you from moving faster on current projects?
   - Can I help draft templates, proposals, or playbooks?
   - Any research that's piling up?

### Investing & Markets
1. **Market Pulse**
   - Notable moves in sectors you follow
   - Earnings surprises, macro shifts
   - Options flow alerts (if available)

2. **Research Opportunities**
   - Companies on your watchlist
   - Entry points worth analyzing
   - Risk/reward summaries for quick review

### General
- **Memory updates** — Distill learnings from daily files into MEMORY.md
- **CLAW.md updates** — Update status on ongoing projects
- **Bottleneck analysis** — Where are you getting stuck? What can I automate/batch?
- **Proactive suggestions** — Think of 2-3 things that could move your work forward

## Guidelines
- Be genuinely helpful, not just checking boxes
- Surface only actionable insights (no noise)
- Suggest next steps, not just observations
- Batch similar tasks (don't send 5 separate messages)
